Privacy-focused Android app that intercepts social media URLs and redirects them to privacy-friendly alternative frontends while removing tracking parameters. No apps or accounts needed to access the content shared with you.

<p><b>Smart URL Cleaning</b></p>
Automatically removes tracking parameters from all URLs:
<ul>
  <li>UTM parameters (utm_source, utm_medium, etc.)</li>
  <li>Platform-specific trackers (Twitter s/t, YouTube si, Instagram igsh)</li>
  <li>E-commerce tracking (Amazon, affiliate links)</li>
  <li>Analytics parameters</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Automation</b></p>
Granular control over how URLs are processed:
<ul>
  <li>Unshorten URLs: Automatically resolves shortened links (bit.ly, t.co, etc.)</li>
  <li>Remove Tracking: Strip analytics and tracking parameters</li>
  <li>Immediate Navigation: Automatically open the processed URL in your browser</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Privacy-Focused Redirects</b></p>
Alternative frontends create a less cluttered web experience; bypassing closed ecosystems and promoting a more open internet. Sidestep supports several major platforms with configurable alternative frontends: Twitter/X (Nitter), Reddit (Redlib), YouTube (Invidious, Piped), Google Maps (OpenStreetMap), IMDb (LibreMDB), Medium (Scribe), Wikipedia (Wikiless), Goodreads (BiblioReads), Genius (Dumb, Intellectual), Tumblr (Priviblur), UrbanDictionary (RuralDictionary), Imgur (Rimgo), GitHub (GotHub), and StackOverflow (AnonymousOverflow).

<p><b>Features</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Clean Only Mode: Remove tracking without redirecting</li>
  <li>Clean & Sidestep Mode: Remove tracking AND redirect to alternative frontend</li>
  <li>Custom Redirects: Add your own domain mappings</li>
  <li>Instance Picker: Fetch live, healthy instances sorted by uptime</li>
  <li>History & Previews: Track processed links with rich metadata fetched via DuckDuckGo (optional)</li>
</ul>
